# Analytics & Forecasting

## Overview

This folder contains the analytics and forecasting components of the SAP S/4HANA-inspired ERP implementation.

The objective of this phase is to transform historical operational and financial data into actionable insights through statistical analysis, forecasting models, and predictive decision-support tools.

## Scope

The implementation focuses on forecasting business performance and identifying trends that support planning and operational decision-making.

Key areas include:

* Revenue Forecasting
* Cash Flow Forecasting
* Seasonal Pattern Analysis
* Accounts Receivable Collection Forecasting
* Predictive Business Analytics

## SAP References

The project is inspired by forecasting and planning capabilities commonly associated with:

* SAP Analytics Cloud (SAC)
* SAP Integrated Business Planning (IBP)
* SAP Business Intelligence Solutions

## Key Features

### Revenue Forecasting

* Monthly revenue forecasting
* Trend and seasonality analysis
* Forecast accuracy evaluation

### Cash Flow Forecasting

* Expected cash inflow prediction
* Collection pattern analysis
* Short-term financial planning support

### Seasonal Analysis

* Identification of peak and low seasons
* Trend decomposition
* Seasonal business performance insights

### AR Collection Forecasting

* Outstanding receivables prediction
* Collection timing estimation
* DSO trend analysis

### Predictive Analytics

* Business performance forecasting
* KPI trend analysis
* Decision-support insights

## Expected Deliverables

* Predictive analytics dashboards
