# KPI Formula Catalog

| KPI name | Formula | Source/output file | ERP/SAP module | Business meaning | Management decision supported | Limitation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| As-of-date collection rate | Payments with `payment_date <= 2025-12-31` / invoices with `invoice_date <= 2025-12-31` × 100 | `09_Documentation/kpi_summary.csv` | FI | Shows cash collected for the reporting position only. | Set credit-control and cash conversion priorities. | Simplified clearing; excludes disputes and credit memos. |
| Open AR as of date | Invoice amount minus payments posted on or before 2025-12-31 | `03_FI_Module/outputs/ar_aging.csv` | FI | Outstanding receivables at the reporting cut-off. | Prioritize dunning, escalation, and cash planning. | Future-dated payments remain source data but are not current cash. |
| Invoice status count | Count invoices by calculated status from paid amount as of date and due date | `03_FI_Module/outputs/invoice_status_summary.csv` | FI | Separates cleared, partially paid, not-yet-due, and overdue invoices. | Focus collectors on overdue and partially paid invoices. | Does not model formal SAP clearing documents or dispute reason codes. |
| Overdue AR exposure | Sum outstanding balance where due date is on or before 2025-12-31 | `03_FI_Module/outputs/overdue_receivables_summary.csv` | FI | Cash at risk from aged receivables. | Escalate old balances and review credit terms. | No customer credit limit configuration is modeled. |
| Total net revenue | Sum of `net_revenue` | `05_SD_Module/outputs/revenue_by_category.csv`, `09_Documentation/kpi_summary.csv` | SD/FI | Total recognized sales activity in the synthetic dataset. | Review overall commercial performance. | Excludes tax, cancellations, and real subledger detail. |
| Operating profit margin | Operating profit / total net revenue × 100 | `04_CO_Module/outputs/profitability_summary.csv` | CO | Profitability percentage after modeled operating costs. | Assess margin health and departmental accountability. | No allocations, depreciation, or tax. |
| Cost center variance | Actual amount − budget amount | `04_CO_Module/outputs/cost_center_variance.csv` | CO | Budget over/under performance. | Rank departments for review. | Budget assumptions are synthetic. |
| Occupancy rate | Rooms sold / available room nights × 100 | `05_SD_Module/outputs/hospitality_kpis.csv` | SD | Utilization of available rooms. | Manage demand and pricing. | Uses simplified room availability. |
| ADR | Room revenue / rooms sold | `05_SD_Module/outputs/hospitality_kpis.csv` | SD | Average room rate. | Evaluate pricing. | No room-type mix. |
| RevPAR | Room revenue / available room nights | `05_SD_Module/outputs/hospitality_kpis.csv` | SD | Revenue productivity per available room. | Balance occupancy and rate. | Simplified calendar. |
| Vendor delay rate | Delayed deliveries / total deliveries × 100 | `06_MM_Module/outputs/vendor_performance.csv` | MM | Supplier reliability. | Review vendors and alternatives. | Delay causes are not modeled. |
| Inventory stock gap | `reorder_point - closing_stock` for reorder-alert rows | `06_MM_Module/outputs/reorder_alerts.csv` | MM | Quantity shortfall against reorder threshold. | Expedite items with the largest service risk. | No MRP optimization or safety-stock calculation. |
| Inventory alert count | Count rows where closing stock is at or below reorder point | `06_MM_Module/outputs/inventory_risk_summary.csv` | MM | Frequency of reorder exceptions by category. | Prioritize procurement workload. | Alert volume does not measure item criticality. |
| Items at risk | Distinct item count in reorder-alert rows by category | `06_MM_Module/outputs/inventory_risk_summary.csv` | MM | Breadth of stockout exposure. | Protect housekeeping, F&B, and maintenance service levels. | No substitute item logic. |
| Cash collection forecast | Three-month moving average after last actual collection period as of 2025-12-31 | `07_Analytics_Forecasting/outputs/cash_collection_forecast.csv` | FI/Analytics | Directional cash planning baseline. | Compare expected collections with AR follow-up needs. | Not production-grade forecasting; future scheduled payments are labeled separately. |
| Revenue forecast future periods | Naive, moving average, and linear trend baselines for periods after latest actual revenue month | `07_Analytics_Forecasting/outputs/revenue_forecast.csv` | Analytics/SD | Simple demand and revenue planning view. | Support planning discussions without overclaiming precision. | Excludes events, competitor rates, weather, and seasonality controls. |
| MAPE | Average absolute percentage error over holdout months | `07_Analytics_Forecasting/outputs/forecast_metrics.csv` | Analytics | Relative forecast error. | Select a transparent baseline with caution. | Unstable if actuals are near zero. |
