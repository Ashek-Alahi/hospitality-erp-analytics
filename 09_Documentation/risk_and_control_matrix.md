# ERP Risk and Control Matrix

| Risk | Related module | Data evidence | KPI/report used | Business impact | Control or management action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Overdue receivables | FI | Outstanding invoices by aging bucket | AR aging | Cash flow pressure and write-off risk | Prioritize dunning and dispute review. |
| Low collection rate | FI | Payments compared with invoices | Collection rate | Weak working-capital conversion | Review payment terms and follow-up cadence. |
| Cost center overspending | CO | Actual cost above budget | Cost center variance | Margin erosion | Department variance review. |
| Revenue concentration by channel | SD | Revenue by channel | Channel report | Margin and demand concentration risk | Review channel strategy. |
| Weak customer segment mix | SD | Revenue by segment | Segment report | Commercial dependence | Adjust sales targeting. |
| Vendor delivery delay | MM | Received date after promised date | Vendor delay rate | Service disruption risk | Vendor corrective action or alternate source. |
| Inventory below reorder point | MM | Closing stock <= reorder point | Reorder alerts | Stockout risk | Replenish or expedite. |
| Poor cash collection planning | FI/Analytics | Historical collections and forecast baseline | Cash collection forecast | Planning uncertainty | Use rolling forecast and AR review. |
| Weak forecast reliability | Analytics | MAPE and MAE | Forecast metrics | Overconfidence in planning | Treat forecasts as baselines only. |
| Simplified synthetic data risk | All | Deterministic prototype data | Scope notes | Misinterpretation as live ERP evidence | Clearly state synthetic data and prototype scope. |
