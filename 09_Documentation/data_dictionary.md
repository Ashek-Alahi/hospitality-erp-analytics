# Data Dictionary

| Dataset | File name | Business purpose | Primary key or grain | Important columns | Related module | Joins/relationships | Example business question supported |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Calendar | `calendar_clean.csv` | Date reference for repeatable reporting periods. | One row per date/month reference. | `date`, `period`, calendar attributes | All | Joins to transaction dates when needed. | Which month does a transaction belong to? |
| Customers | `customers_clean.csv` | Synthetic customer master for segment analysis. | `customer_id` | `customer_id`, `customer_name`, `segment` | SD/FI | Joins to sales and invoices by `customer_id`. | Which customer segments generate revenue? |
| Vendors | `vendors_clean.csv` | Synthetic vendor master for procurement analysis. | `vendor_id` | `vendor_id`, `vendor_name`, category attributes | MM | Joins to procurement by `vendor_id`. | Which vendors have delivery issues? |
| Sales revenue | `sales_revenue_clean.csv` | Revenue transactions by date, channel, category, and customer. | One revenue transaction row. | `sale_date`, `customer_id`, `channel`, `revenue_category`, `rooms_sold`, `net_revenue` | SD/FI | Joins to customers by `customer_id`; aggregated for revenue KPIs. | Which channels and categories drive revenue? |
| Customer invoices | `customer_invoices_clean.csv` | FI-style invoice records for receivables. | `invoice_id` | `invoice_id`, `customer_id`, `invoice_date`, `due_date`, `invoice_amount` | FI | Joins to payments by `invoice_id` and customers by `customer_id`. | Which invoices are overdue? |
| Customer payments | `customer_payments_clean.csv` | Incoming payments against invoices. | One payment row. | `payment_id`, `invoice_id`, `payment_date`, `payment_amount` | FI | Joins to invoices by `invoice_id`. | How much invoiced value has been collected? |
| Cost center budget actual | `cost_center_budget_actual_clean.csv` | Budget and actual costs by cost center. | Cost center and period. | `cost_center_id`, `cost_center_name`, `budget_amount`, `actual_amount` | CO | Aggregated by cost center for variance reporting. | Which departments overspent? |
| Procurement vendor | `procurement_vendor_clean.csv` | Purchase activity and vendor delivery timing. | One procurement transaction row. | `vendor_id`, `category`, `purchase_amount`, `promised_date`, `received_date` | MM | Joins to vendors by `vendor_id`. | Which vendors are late and where is spend concentrated? |
| Inventory movements | `inventory_movements_clean.csv` | Closing stock and reorder-point monitoring. | Item and period. | `period`, `item_id`, `item_name`, `category`, `closing_stock`, `reorder_point` | MM | Used to produce reorder alerts by item/category. | Which items are below reorder point? |
