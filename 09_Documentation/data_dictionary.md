# Data Dictionary

| Dataset | File name | Business purpose | Grain | Primary key | Important columns | Related module | Relationships | Generated output files | Example business question |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Calendar | `02_Data/processed/calendar_clean.csv` | Date reference for repeatable reporting periods. | One row per calendar date. | `date` | `year`, `month`, `quarter`, `day_of_week`, `is_weekend` | All | Can align transaction dates to periods. | SQL date analysis outputs. | Which period does each transaction belong to? |
| Customers | `02_Data/processed/customers_clean.csv` | Synthetic customer master for segment and credit review. | One row per customer. | `customer_id` | `customer_name`, `segment`, `region` | SD/FI | Joins to sales and invoices by `customer_id`. | Customer segment revenue, FI receivables. | Which segments drive revenue and AR exposure? |
| Vendors | `02_Data/processed/vendors_clean.csv` | Synthetic vendor master for procurement analytics. | One row per vendor. | `vendor_id` | `vendor_name`, `category`, `on_time_delivery_pct` | MM | Joins to procurement by `vendor_id`. | Vendor performance. | Which vendors create delivery risk? |
| Sales revenue | `02_Data/processed/sales_revenue_clean.csv` | Revenue transactions by date, channel, category, and customer. | One sales revenue row per category/channel/customer transaction. | `sale_id` | `sale_date`, `customer_id`, `channel`, `revenue_category`, `rooms_sold`, `net_revenue` | SD/FI | Joins to customers by `customer_id`; aggregated by month for trends and forecasts. | Revenue by category, revenue by channel, segment revenue, hospitality KPIs, revenue forecast. | Which channels and categories drive revenue? |
| Customer invoices | `02_Data/processed/customer_invoices_clean.csv` | FI-style invoice records for receivables and invoice-to-cash review. | One row per invoice. | `invoice_id` | `customer_id`, `invoice_date`, `due_date`, `invoice_amount`, `status` | FI | Joins to payments by `invoice_id` and customers by `customer_id`. | AR aging, overdue receivables, invoice status summary/detail, KPI summary. | Which invoices are overdue or partially paid as of 2025-12-31? |
| Customer payments | `02_Data/processed/customer_payments_clean.csv` | Incoming payments against invoices, including some future-dated synthetic payments. | One row per payment. | `payment_id` | `invoice_id`, `payment_date`, `payment_amount` | FI | Joins to invoices by `invoice_id`; filtered by `payment_date <= 2025-12-31` for FI actuals. | Cash collections, collection rate, invoice status, cash collection forecast. | How much cash was collected by the as-of date? |
| Cost center budget actual | `02_Data/processed/cost_center_budget_actual_clean.csv` | Budget and actual costs by department. | One row per cost center per period. | `period` + `cost_center_id` | `cost_center_name`, `budget_amount`, `actual_amount` | CO | Aggregated by cost center for variance; combined with revenue for profitability. | Cost center variance, profitability summary. | Which departments overspent their budgets? |
| Procurement vendor | `02_Data/processed/procurement_vendor_clean.csv` | Purchase activity and vendor delivery timing. | One row per purchase order. | `po_id` | `vendor_id`, `category`, `purchase_amount`, `promised_date`, `received_date`, `quantity` | MM | Joins to vendors by `vendor_id`; delay is calculated from promised and received dates. | Purchase spend, vendor performance. | Which categories have high spend and which vendors are late? |
| Inventory movements | `02_Data/processed/inventory_movements_clean.csv` | Stock movement and reorder-point monitoring for operating supplies. | One row per item per period. | `period` + `item_id` | `item_name`, `category`, `opening_stock`, `received_qty`, `issued_qty`, `closing_stock`, `reorder_point` | MM | Used to calculate reorder alerts and stock gap. | Reorder alerts, inventory risk summary. | Which items and categories are at stockout risk? |
