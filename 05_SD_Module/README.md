# SD Module — Sales and Distribution Analytics

## Business purpose
This module explains which hospitality revenue streams, booking channels, and customer segments drive top-line performance.

## Input files used
- `02_Data/processed/sales_revenue_clean.csv`
- `02_Data/processed/customers_clean.csv`

## Analysis performed
- Revenue by service category.
- Revenue by sales or booking channel.
- Revenue by customer segment using customer master data.

## Output files generated
- `05_SD_Module/outputs/sd_report.md`
- `05_SD_Module/outputs/revenue_by_category.csv`
- `05_SD_Module/outputs/revenue_by_channel.csv`
- `05_SD_Module/outputs/customer_segment_revenue.csv`
- `05_SD_Module/outputs/revenue_by_channel.svg`

## ERP/SAP relevance
The module is inspired by SAP SD reporting ideas around sales transactions, channels, customer master data, and revenue categories.

## Management decisions supported
- Focus commercial effort on stronger channels and segments.
- Review product or service mix by revenue category.
- Support pricing and promotion discussions with segment evidence.
