# SAP S/4HANA ERP Implementation for Hospitality Industry

## Project Overview

This project aims to design and implement a SAP S/4HANA-inspired ERP solution for hospitality operations in Northern Japan. The implementation focuses on integrating financial management, controlling, sales analytics, procurement, inventory management, and business intelligence into a unified decision-support system.

The project is based on real-world hospitality business processes and combines ERP concepts with data analytics, forecasting, and dashboard reporting.

## SAP Module Coverage

* SAP FI (Financial Accounting)
* SAP CO (Controlling)
* SAP SD (Sales and Distribution)
* SAP MM (Materials Management)

## Key Features

* Accounts Receivable (AR) management and aging analysis
* Revenue tracking and financial reporting
* Cost center accounting and budget monitoring
* Profitability analysis by business segment
* Sales performance analytics
* Procurement and inventory management
* Revenue and cash flow forecasting
* Executive and operational dashboards
* Integrated business intelligence reporting
