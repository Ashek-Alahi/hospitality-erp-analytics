# CO Module - Cost Center and Profitability Analytics

This module provides SAP S/4HANA-inspired controlling analytics for hospitality department review. It compares cost center budget versus actuals, ranks variances, and summarizes profitability.

## Analytics covered
- Cost center budget vs actuals
- Variance amount and variance percentage
- Profitability summary
- Operating profit margin

## Business value
Department managers and controllers can identify unfavorable spending variances, discuss operational drivers, and protect margin without reducing guest service quality.

## Outputs generated
- `outputs/cost_center_variance.csv`
- `outputs/profitability_summary.csv`
- `outputs/co_report.md`

## Limitations
Synthetic data only; no allocation cycles, internal orders, activity types, assessment/distribution rules, or product/customer profitability ledger is modeled.
